I'm an Ph.D. student majoring in Computer Science in National University of Singapore. My research interests include neural networks, and machine learning on graph data.

I received my M.S. in Electronic and Computer Engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology and my B.S. in Information Technology from Southeast University in China.
